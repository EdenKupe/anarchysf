The lives of two sisters as the tech-dependent society they were born into disintegrates and they learn to survive alone in the forests of California. The novel was the subject of a warm <a href="https://greenanarchy.anarchyplanet.org/files/2012/05/greenanarchy22.pdf">review</a> in <i>Green Anarchy</i> #22, Spring 2006.
