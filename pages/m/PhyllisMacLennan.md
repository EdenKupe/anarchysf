An attempt to colonise the remote planet of Irdra is thwarted, thanks to vigorous resistance by the indigenous alien anarchist society, which had rejected government far in its past. Rather reminiscent of Eric Frank Russell. Pleasing in its anti-authoritarianism, explicitly described as anarchism.
