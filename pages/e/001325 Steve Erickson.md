Loosely centred on Sally Hemings, Thomas Jefferson's slave mistress, but veering from historical fiction to alternate history and alternate near future. Jesse Cohn's 2006 _Anarchism and the Crisis of Representation_ summarises the novel, noting that "This disrespect for intellectual property and ontological propriety, as well as the presentation of 'worlds in the plural,'" has been called the "anarchic" in postmodern literature.
