More science fictional than you'd expect from an Arthurian story, with Merlyn viewing things from the future, and drawing analogies with the societies of ants and geese. Written during World War II, the book is vigorously pacifist, and Merlyn himself is explicitly cast as an anarchist: "I am an anarchist, like any other sensible person."
