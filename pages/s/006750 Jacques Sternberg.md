A kind of May '68 2.0, in a France collapsing under pollution and the impact of global warming. Sternberg was a Belgian sf author and anarchist, included in the 2014 _Bottled Wasp Pocket Diary_, where _Mai 86_ is described as a "powerful anti-political dystopia". (In French only.)
