Inventive re-imagining of the Frankenstein theme. Treated at some length in the Transmetropolitan's _An Anarchist's Guide to Frankenstein_, reproduced on the <a href="https://www.anarchistfederation.net/an-anarchists-guide-to-frankenstein/#/">
Anarchist Federation</a> website. The anonymous Transmetropolitan author noted that "Not only is the novel brilliant, it’s insanely funny, and certainly worth reading."

The novel includes a brief reference to Glaswegian anarchist Guy Aldred.
