_Kalki_ is a black comedy in which an American successfully masquerades as the Hindu god Kalki, with devastating effect. Perhaps missing the humour, Zeke <a href="https://seesharppress.wordpress.com/2015/01/05/a-few-favorite-atheist-science-fiction-novels-and-books-on-cults/">Teflon</a> describes the novel as "A terrifying look at religious fanaticism and the use of biological WMDs."

In _Live from Golgotha_ a time-travelling hacker messes with Christianity's foundation story.

Both novels are included in <a href="https://seesharppress.wordpress.com/2015/01/05/a-few-favorite-atheist-science-fiction-novels-and-books-on-cults/">Teflon</a>'s list of notable atheist SF novels.
