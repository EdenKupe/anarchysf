Disney-produced series, part of the Star Wars franchise. The series has had warm responses from anarchists, including <a href="https://theanarchistlibrary.org/library/peter-gelderloos-a-long-time-ago-in-a-revolution-far-far-away">Peter Gelderloos</a> and <a class="text-authors-item" href="https://theanarchistlibrary.org/library/riot-medicine-popsec-andor">Håkan Geijer</a> in 2022, and <a href="https://theanarchistlibrary.org/library/margaret-killjoy-when-there-s-too-much-friction-in-the-air">Margaret Killjoy</a> in 2025, all to be found at theanarchistlibrary.org. There is also a relevant interview with Tony Gilroy at <a href="https://reason.com/podcast/2025/12/23/andor-creator-tony-gilroy-on-bureaucracy-and-the-surveillance-state/">reason.com</a>.
